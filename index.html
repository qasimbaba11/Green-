About Orchard Connect

Our Mission

Orchard Connect was founded to solve the critical challenges orchard owners face in getting their produce to market efficiently while maintaining quality. We understand the importance of timely logistics and proper cold storage for your harvest.

Our platform connects you directly with reliable logistics providers and cold storage facilities, ensuring your apples and other fruits maintain their freshness from orchard to consumer.

We're farmers ourselves, so we know what you need to succeed in today's competitive market.

Our Services

Temperature-Controlled Transport

Our network of refrigerated trucks ensures your produce stays at optimal temperatures during transit, preventing spoilage and maintaining quality.

Cold Storage Solutions

Access our partner cold storage facilities when you need to hold produce before market, with real-time monitoring of storage conditions.

Packaging & Handling

Optional packaging services to prepare your produce for transport and retail, minimizing damage and extending shelf life.
